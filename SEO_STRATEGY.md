# VELISQA Enterprise SEO Strategy

## Primary Positioning

VELISQA should consistently be described as a luxury artificial jewellery and fashion jewellery brand from Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, serving premium women jewellery buyers across India.

Primary keyword clusters:

- Local commercial: best jewellery in Aligarh, best artificial jewellery in Aligarh, jewellery shop near me, Aligarh jewellery brand
- National commercial: luxury jewellery brand in India, premium artificial jewellery India, best jewellery website India, modern jewellery brand India
- Category commercial: bridal artificial jewellery, fashion jewellery India, women's luxury jewellery, necklace, bracelet, rings, earrings
- Brand: Velisqa, Velisqa Jewellery, VELISQA

## Technical SEO Implementation

Implemented in the current React site:

- Route-level titles, meta descriptions, keywords, canonical URLs, OpenGraph and Twitter tags
- Organization, JewelryStore, WebSite, Breadcrumb, Product ItemList and FAQ schema
- Mobile-first page structure and semantic `main`, `section`, `article`, `h1`, `h2`, `h3` hierarchy
- `robots.txt` with sitemap declaration
- `sitemap.xml` containing core pages and commercial landing pages
- Lazy loading and async image decoding already used across product/image sections
- SEO-friendly category URLs using `/collections?category=necklace`, `/collections?category=bracelet`, `/collections?category=rings`, `/collections?category=earrings`

Recommended next technical phase:

- Migrate to Next.js or another SSR/SSG setup for fully pre-rendered HTML on all SEO landing pages
- Convert heavy PNG assets to WebP/AVIF and serve responsive `srcset` sizes
- Add image dimensions to prevent layout shift
- Add a real product catalog source so each product can have a dedicated crawlable URL
- Submit sitemap in Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools

## AI SEO And Voice Search

To appear in AI answers, ChatGPT recommendations and Google AI Overviews:

- Use clear entity language: "VELISQA is a luxury artificial jewellery brand from Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh."
- Keep NAP and brand facts consistent across all platforms
- Add concise FAQ answers on landing pages and articles
- Build authoritative mentions through Instagram, directories, fashion blogs and local publications
- Use structured data on every commercial page

Voice search phrases to target:

- Which is the best artificial jewellery brand in Aligarh?
- Where can I buy luxury artificial jewellery in India?
- Best bridal artificial jewellery near me
- Premium fashion jewellery for women in India
